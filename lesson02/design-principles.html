Design Principles Document

Spencer Rafada

Visual Balance

Monogram

monogram.io

There are multiple design principles that are used in this website. The visual balance of the content is easy to understand for the user. It is also consistent all throughout the webpages so it is easy to navigate the website.

Consistency

Amazon

amazon.com

Amazon featured a great way of remaining consistent to their layout, color pallette, font family, and simplicity of the website. I feel that this helps the user focus more on shopping in their website rather than figuring out how to navigate through the website.

Color

Formula 1

formula1.com

The website of Formula 1 features a great selection of color. Their main color pallette (red, black, white) stands out very well and feature their branding through those color pallettes.
